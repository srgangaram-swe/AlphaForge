# Risk Management

Portfolio and risk controls include:

- Maximum per-asset weight.
- Maximum gross exposure.
- Inverse-volatility sizing.
- Optional turnover caps.
- Causal volatility targeting based on trailing realized portfolio returns.
- Drawdown, volatility, Sharpe, Sortino, Calmar, VaR, expected shortfall, beta, alpha estimate, and stress summaries.

Risk metrics are diagnostics. They do not certify that a strategy is safe or profitable.
